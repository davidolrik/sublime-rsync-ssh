Rsync SSH 1.6.0
---------------

- Added support for specific remote/destination sync.
  Press ⌘⇧11 to and select which remote/destination you want to sync.
  Remotes with just one destination will sync when selected.
  Remotes with multiple destinations will allow you to select one or all
  destinations to sync.
  Note the "enabled" flag is ignored when syncing to a single destination.
